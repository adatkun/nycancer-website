Our Imaging Services

Advanced diagnostic imaging with state-of-the-art technology and expert care

MRI

Detailed images of organs, soft tissues, and bones using magnetic resonance technology without radiation.

Computed Tomography (CT)

Cross-sectional imaging of bones, blood vessels, and soft tissues for accurate diagnosis.

PET/CT Scan

Combined imaging to detect cancer, heart disease, and brain disorders by showing structure and metabolic activity.

Mammography

Digital mammography and 3D tomosynthesis for early breast cancer detection and screening.

Ultrasound

Safe, non-invasive imaging using sound waves to visualize internal organs and blood vessels.

Bone Density (DEXA)

Measure bone mineral density to diagnose osteoporosis and assess fracture risk.

Lung Screening

Low-dose CT lung cancer screening for early detection in high-risk patients.

Interventional Radiology & Oncology

Minimally invasive, image-guided procedures for diagnosis and treatment.

Radionuclide Therapies

Targeted radiation therapy using radioactive substances to treat certain cancers.

Echocardiogram (TTE)

Non-invasive ultrasound of the heart to evaluate function and detect heart disease.
