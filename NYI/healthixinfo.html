Healthix Authorization

Authorization for Access to Patient Information through the Healthix Health Information Exchange

What is Healthix?

Healthix is New York's largest health information exchange, connecting healthcare providers across the region to securely share your electronic health information. This helps ensure your doctors have access to your complete medical history for better, more coordinated care.

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

This document provides details about the information accessed through Healthix and the consent process. By providing your consent, you authorize New York Imaging Specialists to access your electronic health information through the Healthix health information exchange network.

1. How Your Information May Be Used

Your electronic health information will be used only for the following healthcare services:

Treatment Services

Provide you with medical treatment and related services, including diagnostic imaging procedures such as MRI, CT, PET/CT, and other imaging studies.

Insurance Eligibility Verification

Check whether you have health insurance and what imaging services and procedures it covers.

Care Management Activities

Assist you in obtaining appropriate medical care, improve the quality of services provided to you, coordinate the provision of multiple health care services, and support you in following a medical care plan.

Quality Improvement Activities

Evaluate and improve the quality of medical care and imaging services provided to you and all patients.

2. What Types of Information About You Are Included

If you consent, the Provider Organization(s) listed may access ALL of your electronic health information through Healthix. This includes information created both before and after the date this form is signed.

Your health records may include a history of illnesses or injuries you have had (like diabetes or a broken bone), test results (like X-rays or blood tests), and lists of medicines you have taken.

This information may include sensitive health conditions, including but not limited to:

Medical History
  • Diagnostic Information
  • Clinical Notes
  • Discharge Summary
  • Lab Tests
  • Allergies
Medications & Treatment
  • Medication and Dosages
  • Substance Use History Summaries
  • Alcohol or Drug Use Problems
  • Mental Health Conditions
Sensitive Information
  • HIV/AIDS
  • Sexually Transmitted Diseases
  • Genetic (Inherited) Diseases or Tests
  • Birth Control and Abortion (Family Planning)
Administrative Data
  • Claims Encounter Data
  • Employment Information
  • Living Situation
  • Social Supports

4. Who May Access Information About You, If You Give Consent

Only doctors and other staff members of the Organization(s) to whom you have given consent to access—who carry out activities permitted by this form as described in Section 1—may access your health information through Healthix.

Your Information Is Protected

Access to your health information is strictly limited to authorized healthcare professionals who are directly involved in your care at organizations you have consented to share with.

5. Public Health and Organ Procurement Organization Access

Federal, state, or local public health agencies and certain organ procurement organizations are authorized by law to access health information without a patient's consent for certain public health and organ transplant purposes.

Important Notice

These entities may access your information through Healthix for these purposes without regard to whether you give consent, deny consent, or do not fill out a consent form. This access is required by law to protect public health and facilitate life-saving organ transplants.

7. Re-disclosure of Information

Any organization(s) to whom you have given consent to access health information may re-disclose your health information, but only to the extent permitted by state and federal laws and regulations.

Special Protections for Sensitive Information

Alcohol/drug treatment-related information or confidential HIV-related information may only be accessed and may only be re-disclosed if accompanied by the required statements regarding the prohibition of re-disclosure.

8. Effective Period

This Consent Form will remain in effect until:

You Change Your Consent Choice

You can update your consent preferences at any time

Death

The consent expires upon death of the patient

Healthix Ceases Operation

If Healthix stops operating as a health information exchange

If Healthix merges with another Qualified Entity, your consent choices will remain effective with the newly merged entity.

9. Changing Your Consent Choice

You can change your consent choice at any time and for any Provider Organization or Health Plan by submitting a new Consent Form with your new choice.

Important Information About Changing Consent

Organizations accessing your health information through Healthix while your consent is in effect may copy or include your information in their medical records. Even if you later decide to change your consent, they are not required to return your information or remove it from their records.

How to Change Your Consent

  1. Request a new Consent Form from any of our locations or download it from our website
  2. Complete the form with your updated consent preferences
  3. Submit the form to our Patient Services department
  4. Your new consent choice will take effect upon processing

10. Copy of Form

You are entitled to get a copy of your signed Consent Form at any time. To request a copy, please contact our Patient Services department at any of our locations, or call us at (631) 751-3000.
