Our Comprehensive Pharmacy Services

Specialty Medication Management

Cancer treatments often involve specialized medications that require careful handling and administration. Our pharmacy team has expertise in oncology medications and can provide guidance on proper usage, potential side effects, and drug interactions. We stock a wide range of specialty cancer medications, including:

  • Oral chemotherapy agents
  • Targeted therapy medications
  • Immunotherapy support medications
  • Supportive care medications to manage side effects

Synchronized Care Coordination

Our pharmacy services are fully integrated with your oncology care. This coordination ensures:

  • Timely medication dispensing aligned with your treatment schedule
  • Immediate communication with your physician about any medication issues
  • Proactive monitoring of refill needs to prevent treatment interruptions
  • Comprehensive medication reviews to prevent potential interactions

Medication Compliance Support

Adhering to medication schedules is crucial for treatment success. Our pharmacy team provides:

  • Clear instructions on when and how to take your medications
  • Medication calendars and reminder systems
  • Regular check-ins to address any difficulties with your medication regimen
  • Educational resources about your specific medications

Financial Navigation for Medications

We understand that medication costs can be a significant concern. Our pharmacy works closely with our Financial Assistance team to:

  • Identify manufacturer assistance programs for high-cost medications
  • Connect you with foundation support for medication expenses
  • Navigate insurance coverage and prior authorization requirements
  • Explore all available options to minimize your out-of-pocket costs

Foundation Support and Manufacturer Partnerships

We work with many foundations to obtain funding and provide support for our patients. Many newer cancer medications are difficult to obtain at local pharmacies and even some specialty pharmacies. We have established strong relationships with manufacturers to ensure access to these therapies within our clinics.

Our partnerships with pharmaceutical companies and foundations help us address the financial challenges of cancer treatment. These relationships allow us to:

  • Access patient assistance programs for those who qualify
  • Obtain co-pay cards and discounts from manufacturers
  • Secure grants from nonprofit organizations for medication costs
  • Provide certain medications at reduced or no cost when possible

With our comprehensive network and dedicated team at NYCBS, we can assure that we provide excellent quality and service to our patients throughout their medication journey.
