Patient Care

Comprehensive, compassionate care for patients with cancer and blood disorders.

At New York Cancer & Blood Specialists, we are committed to providing patient-centered care that addresses not only the medical aspects of cancer and blood disorders but also the emotional, practical, and financial challenges that patients and their families face. We combine clinical excellence with compassion to support you through every step of your treatment journey.

Our Patient-Centered Approach

Our approach to patient care is based on the understanding that each patient is unique, with individual needs, preferences, and circumstances. We believe in:

  • Personalized medicine - Tailoring treatment plans to your specific diagnosis, genetics, and personal circumstances
  • Multidisciplinary collaboration - Bringing together experts from various specialties to provide comprehensive care
  • Shared decision-making - Ensuring you are an active participant in decisions about your treatment
  • Accessibility - Making expert care available in your community with extended hours and multiple locations
  • Coordinated care - Streamlining your experience across all aspects of treatment
  • Whole-person care - Addressing physical, emotional, and practical concerns

What to Expect as a Patient

We strive to make your experience as smooth as possible. Here's what you can expect when you come to New York Cancer & Blood Specialists:

Your First Visit

  • Preparation - We'll provide detailed instructions about what to bring (insurance information, medical records, list of medications)
  • Check-in - Our welcoming front desk staff will guide you through the registration process
  • Comprehensive assessment - Your provider will take a complete medical history and perform a thorough examination
  • Discussion - You'll have time to ask questions and discuss concerns
  • Treatment planning - If a diagnosis has been made, initial treatment options will be discussed
  • Next steps - A clear plan for any additional testing or follow-up appointments

Ongoing Care

  • Personalized treatment plan - Developed specifically for your diagnosis and circumstances
  • Regular monitoring - Frequent assessment of your response to treatment and overall well-being
  • Side effect management - Proactive approaches to prevent and address treatment side effects
  • Open communication - Multiple ways to reach your care team with questions or concerns
  • Coordination - Seamless communication among all members of your healthcare team
  • Education - Information and resources to help you understand your condition and treatment

Communication and Access

  • Patient portal - Secure online access to your medical information, test results, and communication with your care team
  • Extended hours - Including evenings and weekends at select locations
  • Multiple locations - Convenient offices throughout New York
  • Urgent care - Same-day appointments for acute issues
  • 24/7 on-call providers - Always available for urgent concerns
  • Telemedicine - Virtual visit options when appropriate
