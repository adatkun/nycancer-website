Chronic Care Management

Coordinated, comprehensive care for patients with multiple chronic conditions.

At New York Cancer & Blood Specialists, we understand that managing multiple chronic health conditions alongside cancer treatment can be challenging. Our Chronic Care Management (CCM) program provides additional support and coordination to ensure all aspects of your health are addressed through a personalized, patient-centered approach.

Comprehensive Care Coordination

The Chronic Care Management Program has been designed to improve the coordination of your health care and create a dedicated team that focuses on your unique health care needs. Our primary goal is to reduce the frequency of urgent and emergency visits for patients with two or more chronic health conditions while providing additional support and counseling for healthcare decisions.

Our CCM program serves as an integral part of our Health & Wellness Program, ensuring that your entire scope of care is being followed throughout your treatment journey. We believe that managing your chronic conditions effectively is essential to your overall treatment success and quality of life.

How Our CCM Program Works

The Chronic Care Management program takes a systematic approach to addressing your healthcare needs through personalized care planning and ongoing support.

Patient-Centered Care Plan

Our CCM team develops a comprehensive, Patient-Centered Care Plan based on a thorough assessment that considers your physical, mental, cognitive, psychosocial, functional, and environmental needs. This holistic approach ensures that all aspects of your health are addressed in a coordinated manner.

Enhanced Access to Care

The program ensures you have plentiful access to care, with multiple opportunities for you and your caregivers to communicate with your healthcare practitioners. This enhanced communication helps address concerns promptly and adjust your care plan as needed.

Systematic Health Management

Our team systematically assesses your medical, functional, and psychosocial needs to manage your care effectively. This comprehensive approach helps identify potential issues before they become serious problems, allowing for proactive intervention.

Coordination with Specialists

We coordinate your care with specialists, community services, and other healthcare providers to ensure a cohesive approach to managing your health. This seamless coordination reduces the burden on you and your family while improving treatment outcomes.

Eligible Chronic Conditions

Our Chronic Care Management program supports patients with multiple chronic conditions. Examples of qualifying chronic conditions include, but are not limited to:

Cancer

Diabetes

Heart Failure

Hypertension

Arthritis

(Osteo & Rheumatoid)

Asthma

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease

Alzheimer's Disease & Related Dementia

Atrial Fibrillation

If you have two or more chronic conditions, you may be eligible for our CCM program. Eligible patients will be contacted by a clinical staff member to review your CCM enrollment.

Components of Your Comprehensive Care Plan

Your Comprehensive Care Plan addresses all health issues and typically includes the following elements:

  • Problem list (diagnosed chronic diseases)
  • Expected outcome and prognosis
  • Measurable treatment goals
  • Symptom management strategies
  • Planned interventions and identification of responsible individuals
  • Medication management
  • Community/Social services ordered
  • Coordination of external specialist services
  • Schedule for periodic review and revision of the care plan

This comprehensive approach ensures that all aspects of your healthcare are considered and managed in a coordinated manner, improving your overall outcomes and quality of life.

Integration with Our Health & Wellness Program

The Chronic Care Management program is a vital component of our Patient Health & Wellness Program at New York Cancer & Blood Specialists. It works alongside other supportive resources to provide comprehensive care throughout your treatment journey.

Complementary Services

In addition to Chronic Care Management, our Health & Wellness Program offers several complementary services to support your overall wellbeing:

  • Behavioral Health: Support for managing psychological aspects of chronic illness
  • Palliative & Supportive Care: Focused on improving quality of life and relieving symptoms
  • Nutrition: Personalized nutritional guidance to support your health goals
  • Social Work: Assistance with resources, support services, and care coordination

These services work together to ensure that all aspects of your health—physical, emotional, and social—are addressed throughout your treatment.
