Blood Cancer Treatment

Specialized care for patients with leukemia, lymphoma, and myeloma.

Blood cancers affect the production and function of blood cells, originating in the bone marrow and lymphatic system. At New York Cancer & Blood Specialists, we provide leading-edge treatments and compassionate, personalized care to support you through every stage of your blood cancer journey.

Understanding Blood Cancers

Blood cancers occur when abnormal blood cells begin to grow out of control, interrupting the function of normal blood cells that fight infection and produce new blood cells. Our specialists work closely with you to develop a personalized treatment plan based on the specific type and characteristics of your blood cancer, your overall health, and your preferences.

Major types of blood cancers include:

  • Leukemia - Cancer of the blood and bone marrow that affects white blood cells
    • Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL)
    • Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML)
    • Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia (CLL)
    • Chronic Myeloid Leukemia (CML)
  • Lymphoma - Cancer that begins in the lymphatic system
    • Hodgkin Lymphoma
    • Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma (numerous subtypes)
  • Multiple Myeloma - Cancer of the plasma cells in bone marrow
  • Myelodysplastic Syndromes (MDS) - Group of disorders caused by poorly formed or dysfunctional blood cells
  • Myeloproliferative Neoplasms - Blood cancers that cause blood cells to grow abnormally

Diagnosis and Testing

Accurate diagnosis is essential for effective treatment. We offer comprehensive diagnostic services including:

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC) - Measures levels of different types of blood cells
  • Bone Marrow Biopsy and Aspiration - Examines bone marrow cells
  • Flow Cytometry - Identifies specific types of blood cells
  • Cytogenetic Analysis - Examines chromosomes in cancer cells
  • Molecular Testing - Identifies genetic mutations in cancer cells
  • Imaging Studies - CT scans, PET scans, and MRIs to determine cancer spread

Treatment Options for Blood Cancers

Our multidisciplinary approach ensures you receive the most effective treatments. Your personalized treatment plan may include one or more of the following options:

Chemotherapy

  • Traditional chemotherapy regimens customized for different blood cancers
  • Combination chemotherapy approaches for enhanced effectiveness
  • Reduced-intensity protocols for elderly or frail patients

Targeted Therapy

  • Monoclonal antibodies that target specific proteins on cancer cells
  • Tyrosine kinase inhibitors for specific genetic mutations
  • Proteasome inhibitors for multiple myeloma
  • BCL-2 inhibitors and other targeted agents

Immunotherapy

  • Immune checkpoint inhibitors to enhance immune system response
  • CAR T-cell therapy for eligible patients
  • Bispecific antibodies targeting multiple cancer proteins
  • Immunomodulating drugs to regulate immune function

Stem Cell Transplantation

  • Autologous transplant (using your own stem cells)
  • Allogeneic transplant (using donor stem cells)
  • Reduced-intensity conditioning regimens for suitable patients
  • Transplant aftercare and monitoring for optimal recovery

Radiation Therapy

  • Targeted radiation for lymphomas and localized disease
  • Total body irradiation as part of transplant preparation
  • Palliative radiation for symptom management

Clinical Trials

We offer access to innovative clinical trials testing the latest treatments for blood cancers. Ask your doctor if you might be eligible for a clinical trial.

Comprehensive Support Services

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the disease. Our support services include:

  • Transfusion services for management of blood cell deficiencies
  • Infection prevention and management strategies for immunocompromised patients
  • Nutrition counseling to help maintain strength during treatment
  • Support groups for connecting with others on a similar journey
  • Pain management and palliative care when needed
  • Psychological support for emotional well-being
  • Financial counseling to navigate insurance and treatment costs
