Insurance Verification

Our team will verify your insurance coverage prior to your first appointment. If you have questions about your specific insurance plan or coverage, please contact our dedicated insurance specialists who can assist you.

Please be prepared to provide the following information when scheduling your appointment:

  • Your insurance card(s)
  • Photo ID
  • Any referral forms required by your insurance
  • Information about your primary care physician

We understand that navigating insurance coverage can be complex, especially during a challenging time. Our financial counselors and benefits specialists are available to help you understand your coverage and explore potential financial assistance options if needed.

Insurance FAQs

What if my insurance plan is not listed? +

If you don't see your insurance plan listed, please contact our insurance specialists at (855) 528-7322. We are continuously expanding our accepted insurance networks and may be able to work with your provider even if they are not currently listed.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist? +

Referral requirements vary by insurance plan. Some plans, particularly HMOs and certain Medicare Advantage plans, require a referral from your primary care physician before seeing a specialist. Please check with your insurance provider about their referral requirements before your appointment.

How do I know what my insurance will cover? +

Coverage varies by insurance plan, even within the same insurance company. Our financial counselors can help you understand your benefits and estimate your out-of-pocket costs. We recommend contacting your insurance provider directly to confirm coverage details for specific procedures or treatments.

What if I don't have insurance? +

For patients without insurance, we offer financial counseling to discuss payment options. Our benefits counselors can also help explore potential eligibility for insurance programs, financial assistance, or payment plans. Please contact our financial services department to discuss your specific situation.
