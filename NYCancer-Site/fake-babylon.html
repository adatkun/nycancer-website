Specialties & Services

Our Babylon location offers comprehensive cancer care with multiple specialties and advanced services under one roof.

Medical Oncology Services

Our medical oncologists provide comprehensive treatment using the latest systemic therapies to combat cancer throughout the body.

Chemotherapy

+

Traditional chemotherapy uses powerful medications to destroy cancer cells throughout the body. Our experienced team administers treatment in comfortable infusion suites with personalized care plans tailored to your specific cancer type and stage.

Immunotherapy

+

Cutting-edge treatments that harness your body's immune system to fight cancer. These therapies help your immune system recognize and attack cancer cells more effectively, offering new hope for many cancer types.

Targeted Therapy

+

Precision medications that target specific genetic changes in cancer cells. These treatments are often more effective and have fewer side effects than traditional chemotherapy because they specifically target cancer cells while sparing healthy tissue.

Hormone Therapy

+

Treatment for hormone-sensitive cancers such as breast and prostate cancer. These therapies block or lower the amount of hormones in the body to slow or stop cancer growth.

Clinical Trials

+

Access to cutting-edge treatments through carefully monitored research studies. Clinical trials offer the opportunity to receive innovative therapies that may not yet be widely available.

Survivorship Care

+

Comprehensive follow-up care for cancer survivors, including monitoring for recurrence, managing late effects of treatment, and promoting overall health and wellness after treatment completion.

Radiation Oncology Services

Our radiation oncology team uses advanced technology to deliver precise, targeted radiation treatments while minimizing exposure to healthy tissue.

External Beam Radiation

+

The most common form of radiation therapy, delivered from outside the body using advanced linear accelerators. Treatment is carefully planned and targeted to maximize effectiveness while protecting healthy tissue.

IMRT (Intensity Modulated Radiation)

+

Advanced radiation technique that allows precise control of radiation dose intensity, conforming treatment to the exact shape of the tumor while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy organs.

3D Conformal Radiation

+

Three-dimensional treatment planning that shapes radiation beams to match the exact contours of the tumor, providing more precise targeting than traditional radiation methods.

Brachytherapy

+

Internal radiation therapy where radioactive sources are placed directly inside or near the tumor. This allows delivery of high doses of radiation to cancer cells while limiting exposure to healthy tissue.

Stereotactic Radiosurgery

+

Highly precise radiation treatment that delivers large doses of radiation to small, well-defined tumors in a single or few treatment sessions. Despite the name, no surgery is involved.

Treatment Planning

+

Comprehensive treatment planning using advanced imaging and computer modeling to determine the optimal radiation dose, angles, and timing for each individual patient's treatment.

Urology Services

Our urologic oncologists specialize in treating cancers of the urinary system and male reproductive organs with advanced surgical and non-surgical approaches.

Prostate Cancer Treatment

+

Comprehensive prostate cancer care including active surveillance, surgery, radiation therapy, and hormone therapy. We offer the latest treatment options tailored to each patient's specific situation and preferences.

Bladder Cancer Care

+

Advanced treatment for bladder cancer including transurethral resection, intravesical therapy, radical cystectomy, and bladder reconstruction procedures using minimally invasive techniques when appropriate.

Kidney Cancer Treatment

+

Treatment for renal cell carcinoma and other kidney cancers using surgical approaches including partial nephrectomy, radical nephrectomy, and targeted therapies for advanced disease.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

+

Laparoscopic and other minimally invasive surgical techniques that result in smaller incisions, less pain, shorter hospital stays, and faster recovery times compared to traditional open surgery.

Robotic Surgery

+

State-of-the-art robotic-assisted surgery providing enhanced precision, improved visualization, and greater surgical control for complex urologic procedures with minimal invasiveness.

Urologic Oncology

+

Specialized care for all cancers affecting the urinary system and male reproductive organs, including testicular cancer, penile cancer, and rare urologic malignancies.

Advanced Imaging Services

Our comprehensive imaging services use the latest technology to provide accurate diagnosis, staging, and monitoring of cancer treatment response.

CT Scans

+

High-resolution computed tomography scans that provide detailed cross-sectional images of the body, essential for cancer diagnosis, staging, and treatment planning.

MRI

+

Magnetic resonance imaging using powerful magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of organs and tissues, particularly useful for brain, spine, and soft tissue evaluation.

PET/CT Scans

+

Combined positron emission tomography and computed tomography that shows both the structure and function of organs, providing precise information about cancer location and metabolic activity.

Ultrasound

+

Non-invasive imaging using sound waves to create real-time images of organs and tissues, commonly used for guided biopsies and monitoring treatment response.

Mammography

+

Specialized breast imaging including 3D mammography (tomosynthesis) for enhanced breast cancer screening and diagnostic evaluation with improved accuracy and reduced false positives.

DEXA Scans

+

Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry to measure bone density and assess fracture risk, particularly important for cancer patients receiving treatments that may affect bone health.
