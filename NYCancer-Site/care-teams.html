Your Care Team Members

Each Care Team at New York Cancer & Blood Specialists is carefully structured to provide comprehensive support. Led by your oncologist, your team includes clinical professionals and support staff who work together seamlessly to coordinate all aspects of your care.

Board-Certified Oncologist

Your oncologist directs your overall treatment plan, drawing on specialized expertise in cancer diagnosis and treatment. They lead your Care Team, making critical medical decisions while considering your personal preferences and quality of life. Your oncologist maintains close communication with all team members to ensure coordinated, consistent care.

Advanced Practice Providers

Our nurse practitioners and physician assistants work closely with your oncologist to monitor your condition, manage symptoms, adjust medications, and provide timely care between physician visits. These highly trained professionals have specialized knowledge in oncology and play a vital role in your day-to-day care, offering both clinical expertise and compassionate support.

Administrative Assistants

Your administrative assistant serves as your primary point of contact for scheduling, paperwork, and navigating the healthcare system. They coordinate appointments, process referrals, manage prescription refills, and help resolve insurance questions. These team members ensure that logistical aspects of your care proceed smoothly, allowing you to focus on your treatment and recovery.

Nutritionists

Cancer and its treatments can significantly impact your nutritional needs and eating habits. Our registered dietitians provide personalized nutritional guidance to help maintain your strength during treatment, manage side effects, and support your overall health. They develop customized eating plans that address your specific challenges and preferences.

Wellness Specialists

Our wellness specialists focus on supporting your physical and emotional well-being throughout treatment. They provide guidance on exercise, stress management, and complementary therapies that can enhance your quality of life and treatment outcomes. These specialists help you develop strategies to maintain your energy, manage anxiety, and preserve your sense of normalcy during treatment.

How to Connect With Your Care Team

Each physician's Care Team has a dedicated email address and phone number for patient communication. These contact details ensure you can reach your Care Team directly whenever you need assistance or have questions about your treatment.

Your Physician's Care Team Card

During your first visit, you will receive your physician's Care Team card which contains the dedicated email address and phone number for direct communication with your team. If you need this information at any time, please ask any front desk worker for your physician's Care Team card.

Patient Portal

Our secure patient portal offers another convenient way to communicate with your Care Team. Through the portal, you can send messages, request prescription refills, view appointments, access test results, and review your treatment plan.

When to Contact Your Care Team

Your Care Team is available to address questions about your treatment plan, manage symptoms, coordinate appointments, discuss nutritional concerns, or provide guidance on any aspect of your care. For medical emergencies, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.
