About Us

Providing world-class cancer and blood disorder care in our communities.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide world-class, patient-centered, affordable care to patients with cancer and blood disorders in their own communities, close to family and friends.

Our vision is a world free of cancer and blood disorders by contributing to the search for cures by offering cutting-edge clinical trials and personalized, state-of-the-art treatments that save and prolong lives.

With locations in Nassau and Suffolk counties, as well as in the Bronx, Manhattan, Queens, Staten Island, Brooklyn, and Upstate New York, NYCBS brings advanced cancer and blood disorder treatment within reach of the communities we serve. We understand the significance of being close to home during these challenging times and strive to make this journey as manageable as possible for our patients.

Comprehensive Care, Compassionate Approach

At NYCBS, we approach cancer and blood disorder care holistically, with a commitment to treating the whole patient, not just the disease. Our multidisciplinary and comprehensive approach to care utilizes the most advanced imaging, state-of-the-art therapies, including medical oncology and radiation oncology, as well as surgery. We believe in addressing the emotional, psychological, and physical aspects of your well-being and offer a wide range of supportive services, including nutrition, social work, psychology, physical therapy, chronic care management, financial assistance, and more.

NYCBS is at the forefront of medical technology and equipment. Our full-service research department and dedicated team of specialists continuously work towards advancing cancer care. Patients can access more than 60 clinical trials featuring the latest cancer therapeutics, as well as an on-site laboratory and pharmacy, making care as convenient as possible.

Our Core Belief

Advocating for and ensuring the health and well-being of our patients is and always will be our top priority. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care, guided by our core values of compassion, respect, and excellence. As your trusted partners in cancer care, we aim to provide not only effective treatment but also the support, understanding, and comfort you deserve.

Thank you for choosing New York Cancer & Blood Specialists. We are honored to stand by your side every step of the way in the fight against cancer and blood disorders.

Patient Advocacy

In the advent of sequestration, advocating for patient choice is a natural extension of our continuing commitment to patient well-being.

We believe that every patient deserves access to the highest quality care, regardless of their circumstances. Our patient advocacy program works to ensure that patients have the resources and support they need throughout their cancer journey.

Our team works diligently to help patients navigate insurance challenges, financial concerns, and other barriers to care, ensuring that treatment decisions are based on medical needs rather than external constraints.
