Appointments

If you or someone you know needs an appointment, you can call our Physician Referral Service to make an appointment at New York Cancer & Blood Specialists.

You do not need a referral from a doctor to contact us or to meet with one of our experts.

To speak with our New Patient Navigator, please call 631-675-5059 or submit an appointment request here.

When calling for your first appointment at New York Cancer & Blood Specialists, you will be invited to speak with our New Patient Navigator. He or she will arrange your visit by acquiring all of your information and medical records, and by scheduling your appointment.

Your New Patient Navigator will also guide you on what is needed on your first visit, and explain what to expect upon your arrival.

The information you will need on-hand when you call is:

  • Full name
  • Contact information
  • Date of birth
  • Diagnosis
  • Results of biopsies, x-rays, or other diagnostic studies
  • Information about prior treatment for your current illness
  • Primary medical insurance carrier
