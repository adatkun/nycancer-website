Clinical Trials at NYCBS

New York Cancer & Blood Specialists is proud to offer one of the region's most comprehensive clinical trials programs. Our current portfolio includes studies investigating novel treatments for various cancer types and blood disorders at different stages.

Clinical trials provide patients with access to promising new treatments before they're widely available. By participating in a clinical trial, you not only gain access to cutting-edge therapies but also contribute to the advancement of cancer care for future patients.

Our research team carefully selects trials based on scientific merit and potential benefit to our patient population. Each trial undergoes rigorous review to ensure it meets our high standards for safety and scientific integrity.
